Start Your Journey at Hindusthan Educational Institutions

At Hindusthan, we make your path to admission smooth, transparent, and accessible. Our online application process is designed to guide you step by step—from registration to final submission.

Whether you're applying for undergraduate or postgraduate studies, follow these five essential steps to join a legacy of excellence and innovation.

01

Register Yourself

Create your account by entering basic details like your name, email, and mobile number. This helps initiate your application journey securely.

02

Verify Email

A verification link will be sent to your registered email. Click the link to activate your account and continue the application process.

03

Fill Application Form Online

Complete the online application form with your academic, personal, and course-related information. Ensure all details are accurate and up to date.

04

Pay Registration Fee

Proceed to pay the non-refundable registration fee through the secure payment gateway. This confirms your intent to apply for the course.

05

Submit Application

Review all entered information carefully and submit your application. You will receive a confirmation message and application reference number.

Graduate Diploma Requirements:

Open to students 18 years and over at course commencement

If English is not your first language, you will need to demonstrate English language proficiency equivalent to IELTS (Academic) 6.0 overall, with a minimum of 5.5 in each band and 6.0 in speaking.

All applicants are interviewed as part of the admissions process.

Applicants not meeting the entry criteria may be considered on a case by case basis by the Academic Director. Consideration will be given to those with appropriate and evidenced previous academic history, prior work experience and life skills experience.
