Admission Policy

Supporting academic and administrative efficiency.

Admission Procedure

Guidelines and criteria for admission to various programmes offered by Hindusthan Institute of Technology.

Criteria

(i) Selection Procedure

The selection committee constituted by Hindusthan Institute of Technology as per the guidelines of the Institution will do selection of the candidates under this scheme. The selection is purely provisional and always subject to the confirmation from the Anna University / Directorate of Technical Education, Chennai.

(ii) Selection Committee

Selection Committee Constituted by Hindusthan Institute of Technology as per the guidelines of the Institution will select the candidates for admission for various programmes offered by our institution.

(iii) Mode of Selection

BE/BTech Degree Programme – First Year

Based on the cut off marks from the higher secondary (10+2) examination.

Cut off mark = 200 (100 for Mathematics and 100 for Physics + Chemistry)
BE/BTech Degree Programme – Direct Second Year Lateral Entry

Based on the percentage marks obtained in the Diploma (10+3/12+2) examination.

ME/MTech Degree Programme

Based on the entrance (TANCET / CET / GATE) marks conducted by the Anna University & Association of Management of Coimbatore Anna University Affiliated Colleges.

Master of Business Administration (MBA) Degree

Based on the entrance (MAT / TANCET / CET) marks conducted by the Anna University & Association of Management of Coimbatore Anna University Affiliated Colleges.

Hindusthan

Hindusthan Educational and Charitable Trust (HECT) was founded in the year 1992 by the eminent Industrialist and Philanthropist, Shri T.S.R Khannaiyann. The Trust is managed by the Managing Trustee, Smt. Sarasuwathi Khannaiyann, Executive Trustee & Secretary Smt. Priya Satish Prabu and Trustee Shri.K.Sakthivel Raja.

Scholarships

Hindusthan Institute of Technology is committed to support meritorious students and making professional education accessible to the underprivileged students. Educational scholarships, fee waivers and concessions are granted to deserving students with exemplary academic and extra-curricular records.

Management Scholarship

In addition to free education, the management of Hindusthan Institute of Technology gives scholarships to students in a variety of categories, including merit scholarships, talent test scholarships, employee scholarships, sports scholarships and private scholarships.

Merit Scholarship

Merit Scholarships are awarded to students based on the performance in their 12th grade board examination. Scholarships are also extended to the children of the employees of Hindusthan Educational and Charitable Trust (HCET) and to the students who have already studied in Hindusthan Group of Institutions.

Talent Test Scholarship

We at Hindusthan believe that a little encouragement can go a long way in inspiring the students to demonstrate their proficiency, win appreciation and prepare their mind for higher targets in the future. Talent test scholarship is a special initiative designed to encourage students to earn scholarships and fee concessions based on their scores on the annual talent test conducted by Hindusthan Group of Institutions.

Sports Scholarship

The institution also has an excellent financial aid programme in the form of sports scholarships to support budding sportsmen and sportswomen who have represented their institution in the university, state, national and international level of sports and games. The scholarship is extended to subsequent years of study, provided the students have a minimum of 60% attendance, have no arrears and are duly certified by the Physical Education Director.

Private Scholarship

Eligible students from poor economic and social background can also apply for sponsorships from various non-governmental and private welfare organizations like Shanthi Social Services (SSS) and Agaram Foundation etc. to pursue their undergraduate and postgraduate programmes at Hindusthan Institute of Technology.

Free Education

Scholarships under the Free Education Scheme as per the norms of Anna University, Chennai are also provided to students.

Anna University Scheme

Hindusthan offers an inclusive, welcoming campus where students learn with purpose, explore with confidence, and embrace opportunities that shape their future.
