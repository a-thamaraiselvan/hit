Pharmaceutical Technology

(Accredited by NBA)

About Our Department

Pharmaceutical industry is one of the most developing and rapidly growing sectors of global economy. India is known as “Pharmacy of the World”, being a supplier of affordable drugs to many countries due its pivotal position in the global pharmaceutical market. The government of India has adopted initiatives to further promote the pharmaceutical sector in India under Green-Field and Brown-Field Projects. Under these projects, the companies will establish new facilities or utilize the existing plants to start a new production activity which requires a huge number of trained professionals well versed on modern technologies. With a view to catering to this increasing need for qualified professionals in the pharmaceutical industries, a four year B.Tech. - Pharmaceutical Technology programme has been started in the year 2019 with a sanctioned intake of 60 students under Anna University, Chennai. The curriculum of the course is designed in line with emerging concepts in the area of drug discovery, design, drug-lead optimization, production, product characterization, quality control and marketing to produce qualified and competent technologists to take up the challenges of pharmaceutical industry. It is accomplished through the dedicated efforts of a team of highly qualified faculty, state-of-the-art laboratory facilities and a support from industry experts. The members of faculty in the department are committed to disseminate basic and applied knowledge to the students which will lead them to stay ahead in all aspects in the field of pharmaceutical technology. The initiatives at the department will certainly offer a conducive environment for higher education and research among the student community besides promoting their individual and professional development.

Vision and Mission

Transforming individuals into technocrats’ proficient to innovate invent and disseminate knowledge for the socio-economic benefit of the society.

MD1:To offer application-oriented quality education through innovative teaching-learning practices.

MD2:To provide industry driven skill development to the students to become innovative, competent and contributing engineers.

MD3:To encourage and engage faculty and students to carry out constructive and socially relevant research.

MD4:To nurture the entrepreneurial, leadership and ethical qualities of students for self and societal growth.

PSOs, PEOs & POs

PEO 1: Graduates will apply their knowledge and skills in their core engineering and allied areas to create a congenial ambience for synergizing academia, research, technology and industry.

PEO 2: Graduates will demonstrate the understanding of multicultural and global perspectives to work both as team member and leader with a sense of ethics and professionalism.

PEO 3: Graduates will update their knowledge constantly through continual learning to foster their personal and organizational growth.

PSO 1: Apply the fundamental concepts of drug delivery to develop efficacious dosage forms.

PSO 2: Design and develop methods to minimize the emergence of drug resistance in microbes.

PSO 3: Plan and deploy the production of pharmaceuticals from natural resources.

After completion of graduation in Pharmaceutical Technology, the students will be able to demonstrate the ability to:

PO 1: Apply knowledge of mathematics, science and technology in the discipline

PO 2: Identify, formulate, research literature, and analyse complex engineering problems for its solution

PO 3: Design and develop system processes that meet the specified needs with appropriate consideration for public health, safety, cultural, societal, and environmental.

PO 4: Design the experiments, its analysis and interpretation of data, synthesis of the information using research-based knowledge for complex problems.

PO 5: Use modern engineering tools, software and equipment to meet the needs in the area of Pharmaceutical Technology

PO 6: Apply reasoning informed by the contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal, and cultural issues relevant to the professional engineering practices.

PO 7: Apply knowledge of the impact of pharmaceutical technology solutions in a societal and global context

PO 8: Demonstrate ethical principles and commitment to responsibilities and norms of the Pharmaceutical technology practices

PO 9: Work effectively as an individual and as well as member in teams of diversified professionals

PO 10: Communicate effectively

PO 11: Understand the philosophies of project management principles in Pharmaceutical technology

PO 12: Showcase urge for self-education and life-long learning

Laboratories

BIOCHEMISTRY LABORATORY

The biochemistry laboratory is a captivating realm where science, technology, and innovation converge to unravel the intricacies of life at a molecular level. This lab space is where the building blocks of life are dissected, examined, and manipulated to understand the fundamental processes that govern living organisms. Here, in this controlled environment, various biochemical experiments will be accomplished with proper guidance and safety precautions.

Read More

MICROBIOLOGY LABORATORY

The microbiology laboratory is a sanctuary of invisible life, where students peer into a world teeming with microorganisms. It is a realm of acquiring practical knowledge where the tiniest of creatures are brought into focus, allowing students to explore their profound impact on human health. This lab offers a great platform to perform studies on the microorganisms and explore their physiological and anatomical behavior.

Read More

MOLECULAR BIOLOGY & GENETIC ENGINEERING LABORATORY

The Molecular biology and genetic engineering laboratory is well equipped with the most up-to-date standard tools and techniques such as DNA isolation , separation and sequencing. Techniques such as PCR ,ELISA are performed for the purpose of identification and gene manipulation. The laboratory provides a platform for the students to study and explore the findings at gene level. Accurate record-keeping and ethical considerations are paramount in these experiments to ensure responsible genetic modification.

Read More

PHYSICAL PHARMACEUTICS LABORATORY

It is a specialized facility which is dedicated to the analysis, research, and development of solid and semi-solid dosage forms, as well as other physical aspects of pharmaceutical products. This laboratory focuses on the physical characteristics and properties of drug products that aids ease of its formulations.

Read More

ANALYTICAL METHODS AND INSTRUMENTATION LABORATORY

The analytical methods and instrumentation laboratory has a specialized facility that plays a pivotal role in various scientific disciplines, including chemistry, biology, physics, and engineering. Its primary focus is on developing, validating, and applying analytical techniques and utilizing cutting-edge instrumentation for precise measurements and data analysis.

Read More

PHYSIOLOGY AND PHARMACOLOGY LABORATORY

It provides the effective platform to explore the effects of drugs on biological systems. It deals with the study of drug interactions with the body. These experiments are to understand drug mechanisms ensuring safe and effective pharmaceutical interventions

Read More

MEDICINAL CHEMISTRY LABORATORY

In this laboratory, students gain hands-on experience in synthesizing and analyzing pharmaceutical compounds. They learn to design and test potential drugs, mastering techniques such as spectroscopy and chromatography. This lab fosters critical thinking and problem-solving skills, preparing students for careers in drug discovery and development.

Read More

BIOPROCESS ENGINEERING LABORATORY

Bioprocess laboratory provides students with hands-on experience in the preparation, operation of bioreactors as well the production of bio pharmaceuticals. Through practical experiments, students learn to optimize biological processes, cultivate microorganisms, and develop bio products. This lab fosters skills essential for careers in bio pharmaceuticals and prepares students for taking up real world challenges in bio pharmaceutical industries.

Read More

FORMULATION AND DOSAGE FORMS LABORATORY

Formulation and Dosage Forms Laboratory is equipped with the facility where pharmaceuticals and cosmetics are developed and tested. It gives hands-on experience for developing various pharmaceutical formulations such as tablets, capsules and creams, etc. using tablet making machines, ointment filling machines, friability testers, etc.

Read More

BIOPHARMACEUTICS AND PHARMACOKINETICS LABORATORY

Biopharmaceutics deals with drug formulation and delivery, while pharmacokinetics explores how drugs are absorbed, distributed, metabolized, and excreted in the body. The Biopharmaceutics and Pharmacokinetics Laboratory impart the essential knowledge on drug development, optimizing dosing, drug safety and bioavailability.
