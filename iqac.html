Internal Quality Assurance Cell - Committing to Excellence.

HiTech – Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

Committing to excellence through continuous quality monitoring and institutional improvement.

IQAC Vision

To promote quality culture and sustenance in academics and administrative process for holistic development of the institution.

IQAC Mission

  1. To ensure innovative teaching learning practices.
  2. To develop benchmark for monitoring various programs / activities leading to quality improvement.

IQAC Objectives

01

To sustain quality culture in the institution.

02

To develop a system of conscious, consistent and catalytic improvement in overall performance of the institutions.

03

To optimize and integrate modern methods of Teaching and Learning process.

04

To ensure adequacy in maintaining and functioning the support structure and services.

05

To document various programs/activities leading to quality improvement.

06

To organize inter and intra institutional workshops, seminars on quality related themes and promotion of quality circles.

07

To prepare and submit the Annual Quality Assurance Report (AQAR) as per the guidelines and parameters of NAAC.

08

To apply various institutional accreditation and ranking.

Plan of Action

To continuously revise the curriculum as per the New Education Policy with more skill development courses and holistic value based education.
To provide congenial learning environment for holistic development of Students, Faculty and Supporting Staff.
To stimulate the academic environment for promotion of quality in teaching-learning process.
To undertake quality-related research studies, consultancy and training programmes.
To facilitate Faculty and Student Exchange Programmes with Other Academic Institutions and develop more formal linkages through MoUs.
To create awareness and initiate measures for protecting and promoting environment.
To devise techniques to improve Teaching Learning & Evaluation process.
To continue to provide formal education to needy and deserving students and increase Extension activities.

Significant Contributions made by IQAC

Industry Institute interaction has been strengthened. The Institute has got academic collaboration with IBM for CSE, IT, ECE and AI & DS. Faculty members have been trained in 'Nan Muthalvan' Scheme of Tamilnadu Government. Value added courses have been conducted in all programmes in collaboration with reputed Industries.

Continuous training for Faculty members in ICT tools to make teaching learning process more effective. Video lectures were prepared by faculty members and uploaded on the Institution's YouTube Channel.

To inculcate research culture, 12 research projects have been supported under Research seed money scheme. Faculty members published 93 research papers in SCOPUS indexed journals and 42 text books have been published. Extension activities like Plastic free world, Energy conservation, Books donation and tree planting have been initiated.

IQAC initiated academic alliance with online platforms like Coursera, EdX and NPTEL – 375 courses completed by faculty and students. Faculty participated in nearly 700 webinars, 292 FDP/STTPs and over 200 programmes were organized in virtual mode. NBA Status extended up to 2025 for Aeronautical, CSE, ECE and Mechanical Engineering.
